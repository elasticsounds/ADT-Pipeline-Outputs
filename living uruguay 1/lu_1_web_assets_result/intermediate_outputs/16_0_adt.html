Guessing game!

Let's play a game! Come and guess the objects from the bag.

  • There are some objects in the bag.
  • One member of the group goes to the front of the class.
  • He / she touches the object and describes it to his / her group.

For example:

  • It is big. / It is small.
  • It seems to be (fruit).
  • I think you can eat it.

Write the objects your group and the other group guessed.
